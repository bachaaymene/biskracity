About Algeria

Algeria is a country in North Africa. It is the largest country in Africa and the tenth-largest in the world. The capital city is Algiers.

Geography

Algeria is bordered by several countries, including Tunisia, Libya, Niger, Mali, Mauritania, Western Sahara, and Morocco. It has a diverse landscape that includes deserts, mountains, and a Mediterranean coastline.

Culture

Algeria has a rich cultural heritage influenced by its history and various civilizations. It is known for its traditional music, art, and cuisine.
